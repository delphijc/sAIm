# CIS (Creative Intelligence Suite)

## Purpose

The Creative Intelligence Suite provides structured frameworks for creative problem-solving and innovation using proven techniques (Six Thinking Hats, Design Thinking, Five W's, root cause analysis) to transform challenges into innovative, user-centered solutions.

**Agents:** Analyst, Brainstorming Coach (Carson), Design Thinking Coach (Maya), Innovation Oracle (Victor), Master Storyteller (Sophia), Problem Solver (Dr. Quinn)

---

- Six Thinking Hats methodology
- Role-playing exercises and lateral thinking
- Project elicitation with Five W's questioning

**Techniques:**
- **Six Thinking Hats:** White (facts), Red (emotions), Black (critical), Yellow (positive), Green (creative), Blue (control)

## Workflows

### Six Thinking Hats

Structured perspective-taking methodology for analyzing ideas from multiple viewpoints.

**Phases:**
1. **White Hat:** Gather facts and information
2. **Red Hat:** Explore emotions, intuitions, and reactions
3. **Black Hat:** Apply critical judgment and identify risks
4. **Yellow Hat:** Identify positive aspects and benefits
5. **Green Hat:** Generate creative alternatives
6. **Blue Hat:** Review process and synthesize findings

**Output:** Comprehensive idea analysis from all perspectives

---

### Five W's

Systematic questioning technique for project elicitation and problem clarification.

**Questions:**
- **Who:** Stakeholders, users, team members involved
- **What:** Problem, solution, deliverables
- **Where:** Location, environment, context
- **When:** Timeline, dependencies, milestones
- **Why:** Motivation, business drivers, impact

**Output:** Refined project concept and problem statement

---

### Design Thinking Workflow

Complete design thinking process from empathy to testing.

**Phases:**
1. **Empathize:** User interviews, observations, research
2. **Define:** Problem statement, user needs synthesis
3. **Ideate:** Brainstorming, lateral thinking
4. **Prototype:** Wireframes, mockups, interactive prototypes
5. **Test:** User testing, feedback incorporation, iteration

**Output:** Validated user-centered design solution

---

### Innovation Strategy Workflow

Strategic approach to identifying and planning transformative innovation.

**Phases:**
1. **Trend Analysis:** Market and technology trends
2. **Opportunity Identification:** Market gaps and emerging needs
3. **Strategic Positioning:** Competitive differentiation
4. **Roadmap Development:** Implementation planning
5. **Governance Setup:** Innovation management framework

**Output:** Innovation strategy and roadmap

---

### Problem-Solving Workflow

Systematic approach to diagnosing and solving complex problems.

**Phases:**
1. **Problem Definition:** Clear problem statement and scope
2. **Root Cause Analysis:** 5 Whys, fishbone diagrams
3. **Solution Generation:** Multiple solution options
4. **Feasibility Assessment:** Constraint and risk analysis
5. **Implementation Planning:** Solution deployment

**Output:** Actionable solutions with implementation plan

---

### Storytelling Workflow

Narrative framing and communication strategy development.

**Phases:**
1. **Audience Analysis:** Stakeholder identification and needs
2. **Story Framing:** Narrative arc and emotional hooks
3. **Content Development:** Key messaging and support points
4. **Media Planning:** Channel and format selection
5. **Presentation Strategy:** Delivery and engagement tactics

**Output:** Narrative strategy and presentation materials
