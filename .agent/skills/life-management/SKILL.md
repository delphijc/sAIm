# Life Management

## Purpose

The Life Management Skill centralizes personal organization tasks including scheduling, vacation planning, and goal management. Supports **Human 3.0** goals by managing personal constraints and aspirations stored in the TELOS (TS) file.

## Core Capabilities

### Scheduling & Planning
- Appointment scheduling and calendar management
- Time constraint optimization
- Deadline and time slot management

### Goal Management
- Personal goal tracking and progress monitoring
- Constraint management
- Self-actualization support

### Vacation Planning
- Travel itinerary creation
- Resource booking
- Activity planning and reservation coordination

### Task Organization
- Task creation and prioritization
- Deadline management
- Workflow organization

## Workflows

### Schedule Showings
Appointment scheduling with calendar integration and conflict detection.

### Plan Vacation
Generate comprehensive vacation plans with activities, bookings, and logistics.

### Create Task
Create new tasks with prioritization, deadlines, and workflow integration.

### Goal Tracking
Monitor personal goal progress and constraint management.

## Integration Points

- **Input:** User goals, constraints, preferences from TS file
- **Output:** Schedules, plans, task lists, organized workflows
- **Context:** TELOS file, personal constraints, calendar data
- **Tools:** Scheduling tools, calendar APIs, task management systems

## Human 3.0 Alignment

This skill enables the transition to Human 3.0 by:
- Managing complexity of modern life
- Enabling focus on purposeful work
- Supporting self-actualization goals
- Optimizing time and resource allocation
